Playboi Carti Music Videos

Magnolia

One of Playboi Carti's breakout hits, "Magnolia" features his signature style and catchy melodic beat produced by Pierre Bourne. The video showed Carti's vibrant energy and was the beginning of Carti's exposure in the streets of New York City and ultimately was the birth of one of the most legendary rap personas in history. "Cash Carti".
